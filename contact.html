Speaking? Read this first

What we ask every speaker to cover

To keep the series focused on why an approach works, we ask speakers to loosely follow this structure. You know your topic best — pick whatever focus within it feels most valuable.

1

Background

A brief primer on the topic, so everyone starts from the same place.

2

What

A common pitfall — what people usually get wrong.

3

Why

Why the mistake matters, and how it can affect results.

4

How

Practical guidance on getting it right, and the principles to follow instead.

5

Resources

A few pointers you've found genuinely useful for building deeper understanding.

Sessions run for about an hour: roughly 50 minutes of talk, then 10 minutes of open Q&A. Everything is hosted on Zoom, every other Tuesday.
